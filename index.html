The main objective of PARALYMPIC AIDS is that disabled people have problems related to different parts and due to which they cannot communicate or see.

In this idea, we will integrate three solutions for each deaf, dumb and blind i.e, speech to sign, sign to speech and speech to speech which will help them to overcome their disabilities and this will enhance them in order to overcome their problems.
